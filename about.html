Meet Chumi

Chumi is a talented seamstress who has been sewing since she was in sixth grade. She began with a sewing course in the neighborhood, gaining fundamental sewing skills, and soon progressed to take the alterations course from The Sew Pro. She began her career simply, sewing for her friends and family, before quickly expanding to a wider range of clients.

Chumi has six years of experience, and her capabilities range from sewing hems to adjusting wedding gowns. You can come to her for any of your sewing needs, and can be sure that she will not disappoint!
